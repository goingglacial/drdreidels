Coming to San Francisco
this Hanukkah season.

Dr. Dreidel's started during a Hanukkah party. A group of friends were enjoying some latkes while bumpin' some jams. A lightbulb went off: hip-hop and latkes are two things we genuinely love; why not combine them and share that experience with the world?
From there, Isabel Friedman and Joe Wheeler embarked on a journey to create hand-crafted, artisan latkes that are, and just as the songs from our hip-hop heroes, never lacking in flavor.
